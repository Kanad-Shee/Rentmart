Legal Clarification

The Rentmart Damage Waiver Pool is limited to a maximum payout of ₹50,000 per incident. This pool is intended to cover minor mechanical failures and accidental surface damage incurred during standard operating conditions.

Important Notice: In cases of catastrophic loss, theft, total equipment write-off, or gross negligence (including operation by uncertified personnel), the legal and financial responsibility remains strictly between the equipment owner and the renter.

Rentmart acts solely as a facilitator and platform provider. Users are encouraged to maintain independent commercial insurance policies for high-value equipment assets exceeding our standard platform caps.
