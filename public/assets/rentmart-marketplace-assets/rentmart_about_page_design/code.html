How It Works - For Owners

STEP 01

List Your Machine

Upload photos, set your price, and list technical specifications. Our platform makes it easy to showcase your equipment to thousands of local farmers looking for reliable machinery.

STEP 02

Accept Bookings & Verify

Review rental requests from verified members. Our system handles identity verification and insurance checks automatically, so you can rent with complete peace of mind.

STEP 03

Get Paid Safely

Funds are held in secure escrow and released directly to your account upon successful completion of the rental. No chasing payments, no hassles.

How It Works - For Renters

STEP 01

Search & Book Locally

Filter by equipment type, location, and availability. Find the exact tractor, harvester, or attachment you need within your local rural community.

STEP 02

Safe Delivery & Handoff

Coordinate pickup or delivery through our secure messaging system. Complete a digital condition report at handoff to ensure protection for both parties.

STEP 03

Complete the Job

Focus on your harvest or field prep with high-quality, maintained equipment. Return the machine when done and leave a review for the owner.
